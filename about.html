Take Lessons with Holly

About

Seattle's seasonal variety show is back and more festive than ever! Please join us for the 4th Annual Holly Jolly Holiday Show on Saturday, December 7 at Barboza. In typical fashion, the night begins with a showcase of holiday songs sung by a variety of singers while accompanied by pianist Holly Thornton. As the evening continues, you'll be entertained by local comedians, musicians, and drag queens! Stay through the event and party into the night as Barboza plays holiday hits and dance remixes. This cocktail cabaret-like affair encourages you to walk around and mingle throughout the performances. Grab photos and make new friends to the backdrop of first-time performers and seasoned professionals! Tickets start at $20 for the first 100 tickets and move to $25 leading up to the day of the show. And, don't forget to participate in the costume contest by showing off your funky-fresh style for this year's theme: Holifunk. Doors open at 6:30pm, and the show starts at 7pm! We hope to see you there!
Aaron
Aaron is a Minnesota native that has been calling Seattle home for the last five years. When not adventuring in the mountains or attending a rave, you can find him singing with The Esoterics or performing at local churches. Additionally, Aaron plays tuba and baritone for the Redmond Tech Orchestra and is a member of various LGBTQI+ clubs and organizations. He has enjoyed hosting and producing the Holly Jolly Holiday Show and hopes to see you there for the 4th anniversary!
Holly

For over 22 years, Holly has called Seattle home, embracing the rain while nurturing her passion for music and teaching. With 20 years of experience under her belt, she has transformed her love for music into a thriving teaching business called Lydian Studio, right in the heart of Capitol Hill. Holly loves hosting student shows and themed events that showcase her students' talents, believing wholeheartedly in the power of being witnessed within a fun, supportive, creative community.

At Lydian Studio, laughter and smiles abound, offering a musical journey tailored to each individual. Holly caters her lessons to every skill level and interest, ensuring that whether students want to master classical piano or belt out their favorite pop hits, they’ll do so with confidence and flair. So come join us at the Holly Jolly Show—let's make some music and create unforgettable memories along the way!

Bryan

Originally from Toronto, Bryan is a software engineer specializing in web, mobile and iOS development. A passionate advocate for creating safe, inclusive spaces for people of color and queer communities, Bryan has a deep appreciation for music and fostering environments that allow individuals to express themselves and bond through their shared talents.

Bryan has studied under Holly for several years, playing piano, singing, and occasionally playing saxophone. After calling Seattle home for five years, Bryan recently moved to New York, where he continues to explore ways of blending his love for music and technology. His festive spirit and commitment to the Seattle community make him an invaluable part of the Holly Jolly Holiday Show team, and he is excited to continue creating unforgettable experiences for everyone involved.
