About Us

Seattle's seasonal variety show is back and more festive than ever! Please join us for the 3rd Annual Holly Jolly Holiday Show on Thursday, December 14 at Chop Suey.

In typical fashion, the night begins with a showcase of holiday songs sung by a variety of singers while accompanied by pianist Holly Thornton. As the evening continues, you'll be entertained by local comedians, musicians, and drag queens such as First Lady Sable Jones St. James! Rest assured the party will continue into the night with DJ Woodrow Low playing holiday hits and dance remixes.

This cocktail cabaret-like affair encourages you to walk around and mingle throughout the performances. Be sure to explore the venue and shop the maker's market filled with local crafters providing custom gift ideas for friends and family!

Tickets start at $15 through Thanksgiving weekend, November 26. Tickets are $20 from November 27 - December 13, and they are $25 online and at the door on December 14.

Don't forget to show off your second-hand style for this year's theme: Thriftmas. Doors open at 7pm, and the show starts at 8pm!
Aaron
Aaron is a Minnesota native that has been calling Seattle home for the last five years. When not adventuring in the mountains or attending a rave, you can find him singing with The Esoterics or performing at local churches. Additionally, Aaron plays tuba and baritone for the Redmond Tech Orchestra and is a member of various LGBTQI+ clubs and organizations. He has enjoyed hosting and producing the Holly Jolly Holiday Show and hopes to see you there for the 4th anniversary!
